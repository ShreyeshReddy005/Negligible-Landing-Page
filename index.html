Parasitic Urbanism: Modular Rooftop Vertical Densification by T Shreyesh Reddy

What is the Negligible company? Negligible is an innovative PropTech and architecture startup based in India, founded by T Shreyesh Reddy. While the dictionary definition of "negligible" means insignificant, the Negligible company is dedicated to solving the massive crisis of urban land scarcity. Through their flagship architectural system, Project TERRA-1, Negligible specializes in modular rooftop housing, vertical densification, and parasitic urbanism. The firm utilizes existing infrastructure to construct sustainable living hubs, bypassing the need for new land acquisition.
